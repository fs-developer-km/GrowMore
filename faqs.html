AC & Washing Machine

Common reasons for poor AC cooling include low gas (refrigerant) level, dirty filters, a faulty compressor, or a damaged PCB. Our technician will inspect and diagnose the exact issue and recommend the right fix — whether it's a jet service, gas refilling, or a part replacement.

AC jet service involves deep pressure cleaning of the indoor and outdoor unit — removing dust, dirt, and bacteria from filters, coils, and drain pipes. It improves cooling efficiency and air quality. It is recommended at least once a year, ideally before summer season starts.

Water leakage from a Split AC indoor unit is usually caused by a blocked drain pipe, dirty evaporator coil, or improper installation angle. It should not be ignored as it can damage walls and electrical fittings. Our technician will fix the leakage issue at ₹500 labour charge.

This could be due to a faulty drain pump, clogged filter, broken drum belt, or a motor issue. Do not try to fix it yourself as it may cause further damage. Book a service with us and our technician will diagnose and repair it at your doorstep at affordable labour charges.

Yes, we repair all types — semi-automatic top load, fully automatic top load, and fully automatic front load washing machines. Labour charges start from ₹199 for semi/fully automatic top load and ₹250 for front load machines. All major brands are supported.

Refrigerator, Geyser & Chimney

Not necessarily. In most cases, poor refrigerator cooling is caused by low gas (refrigerant), a faulty compressor, dirty condenser coils, or a broken thermostat — all of which can be repaired. Our technician will diagnose the root cause and give you an honest repair estimate before proceeding.

A geyser not heating water is usually caused by a burnt heating element, faulty thermostat, tripped safety valve, or scale buildup inside the tank. We repair all these issues at affordable charges. Geyser labour charge starts from ₹199 and tank cleaning service starts from ₹350.

Geyser tank cleaning is recommended every 1–2 years to remove calcium and mineral deposits (scale) that reduce heating efficiency and can damage the tank. We offer tank cleaning service for 10, 15, 20 litre geysers at ₹450 and 25 litre & above at ₹550.

Chimney deep cleaning with sodium hydroxide (NaOH) is a professional-grade cleaning method that dissolves stubborn grease, oil, and carbon deposits from inside the chimney motor, filters, and duct — areas that regular cleaning cannot reach. It restores full suction power and eliminates odours. We offer basic cleaning at ₹599 and deep cleaning at ₹1099.
